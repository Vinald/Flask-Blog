# Profile Image User Guide

## How to Upload Your Profile Image

### Step 1: Login to Your Account
Navigate to the login page and enter your credentials.

### Step 2: Go to Your Profile
Click on your username in the top-right navigation bar and select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.

### Step 3: Upload Your Image
1. Scroll down to the **"Profile Image"** section
2. Click the **"Choose File"** button
3. Select an image file from your computer
4. Click **"Update Profile"**

### Supported Image Formats
- PNG (.png)
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- GIF (.gif)
- WEBP (.webp)

### File Requirements
- **Maximum file size:** 16MB
- **Recommended size:** 500x500 pixels or larger
- **Format:** Square images work best

### Where Your Profile Image Appears

#### 1. Profile Page
Your profile image appears at the top of your profile page in a large circular format (150x150 pixels).

#### 2. Navigation Bar
When logged in, your profile image appears next to your username in the top-right corner (28x28 pixels).

#### 3. Blog Posts
Your profile image appears next to your name when you:
- Author a blog post (visible on post pages - 32x32 pixels)
- Create posts that appear in the blog index (24x24 pixels)

### No Profile Image?
If you haven't uploaded a profile image, a default user icon (👤) will be displayed instead.

### Changing Your Profile Image
To change your profile image:
1. Go to your Profile page
2. Upload a new image following the steps above
3. Your old image will be automatically replaced

### Troubleshooting

#### "Invalid file type" Error
Make sure your file is one of the supported formats: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, or WEBP.

#### "File too large" Error
Your image file must be under 16MB. Try:
- Using an online image compressor
- Reducing the image resolution
- Converting to a more efficient format (WEBP or JPEG)

#### Image Not Appearing
If your image doesn't appear after uploading:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try uploading again

#### Image Looks Stretched or Cropped
Profile images are displayed in circles. For best results:
- Use square images (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Center the important part of the image
- Avoid images with important details at the edges

### Privacy & Security
- ✅ Only image files are allowed
- ✅ Files are scanned for security
- ✅ Images are stored securely
- ✅ Only you can upload/change your profile image
- ✅ Images are visible to all users (public)

### Tips for Best Results
1. **Use a clear photo** - Faces work best in circular format
2. **Square aspect ratio** - 1:1 ratio prevents unwanted cropping
3. **Good lighting** - Bright, clear images look better
4. **Centered subject** - Keep the main focus in the center
5. **High resolution** - At least 500x500 pixels recommended
6. **Appropriate content** - Keep it professional and appropriate
